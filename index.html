Land of Coffee

Tucked in the foothills of Mullyangiri range, lies the charming city of Chikmagalur. The land of coffee as it is often called attracts thousands of tourists every year for its verdant valleys and green vistas. If you are in Bangalore, Chikmagalur must be high on your weekend bucket list.

Rishikesh River Rafting

The vast expanse of the Great Himalayas in North India, with its towering mountains and gurgling rivers, provides ample options for indulging in exciting adventurous activities. Rishikesh – the holy city of Rishikesh in Uttarakhand is no exception.But it is, indeed, the white river rafting in Rishikesh that attracts the maximum crowd.The holy river Ganges offers many strenuous rapids to the adrenaline junkies who love the thrill of encountering the splashing waves.
